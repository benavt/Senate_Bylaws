# RAC & VGM Meiosis Amendment

## Motion

Rensselaer Union 57th Student Senate  
Date: 4/7/2026 | Motion #57-02

WHEREAS the Student Senate is empowered by the Rensselaer Union Constitution to be the "chief legislative and policy-making body" of the Rensselaer Union, and is charged with "initiating all legislative action in those areas which affect the Union",

WHEREAS the Student Senate does not have any subsidiary body responsible for overseeing its power to legislate in subject areas pertaining to the Union as an organization,

WHEREAS the Student Senate suffers from a lack of the ability to implement long-term projects for the benefit of the Senate's effectiveness, with many internal reform initiatives forgotten and abandoned,

WHEREAS the low institutional memory within the Senate has led to significantly diminished effectiveness, widespread duplication of efforts, and loss of knowledge,

WHEREAS the position of Vice Grand Marshal has historically had roles both as an overseer of the Senate's committee-based advocacy and as an overseer of internal affairs, but this dual role has led to diminished effectiveness in both capacities;

The Rensselaer Union 57th Student Senate hereby RESOLVES to amend the Student Senate Bylaws:

- To delete Article IX, Clause 7, Subclause (b), Item (i), adjusting enumeration accordingly.
- To strike out "Elections Commission" in Article X, Clause 1, subclause (b), replacing it with "Rules and Administration Committee".
- To replace the item under Article III, Clause 2, which reads "Vice Grand Marshal" with two that read "Vice Grand Marshal for External Affairs" and "Vice Grand Marshal for Internal Affairs" respectively.
- To insert "for External Affairs" after "Vice Grand Marshal" in Article III, Clause 5 in all places where it appears.
- To replace Article III, Clause 6 with the following two clauses, adjusting numbering accordingly:
  - The Vice Grand Marshal for External Affairs shall be responsible for:
    - Overseeing the Senate's role in campus advocacy, becoming familiar with the advocacy work of all Senate committees, and serving as an assistant to committee chairs and members on advocacy work;
    - Meeting regularly with the Grand Marshal and Vice Grand Marshal for Internal Affairs to discuss Senate issues;
    - Serving, as may be necessary, as a liaison to members of the Institute administration to set up meetings and coordinate joint advocacy projects between committees;
    - Serving as a substitute for the Grand Marshal at meetings with Institute administrators as may be requested by the Grand Marshal;
    - Providing guidance and assistance in special operations to raise awareness of issues of importance to students, in collaboration with relevant bodies of the Senate;
    - Assisting individual Senators that have been charged with investigating issues raised by petition; and
    - Creating and maintaining documentation to strengthen committee advocacy work.
  - The Vice Grand Marshal for Internal Affairs shall be responsible for:
    - Serving ex-officio as Chair of the Rules and Administration Committee.
    - Organizing training and assisting in the onboarding of new officers;
    - Publicizing meeting times;
    - Meeting regularly with the Grand Marshal and Vice Grand Marshal for External Affairs to discuss Senate issues;
    - Acting as the substitute for the Grand Marshal at any meetings of the Senate that the Grand Marshal cannot attend. Should the Vice Grand Marshal for Internal Affairs hold a voting position, at such a meeting, they shall not count towards quorum and may only vote to break a tie;
    - Overseeing relations between the Senate and other organizations in the Union, including the Union administrative staff; and
    - Ensuring engagement among Senators and continuity between terms of the Senate.
- To insert "either" before "Vice Grand Marshal" in Article III, Clause 9 (presently Article III, Clause 8), Subclause (b), item (i).
- To replace "with both the SCEC chairperson and the Vice Grand Marshal" with "with the SCEC chairperson or designee(s)" in Article IX, Clause 8, subclause (e), item (i).
- To insert the following clause and associated subclauses after Article IX, Clause 15:
  - The Rules and Administration Committee shall be responsible for investigating internal affairs of the Union, including its administration and policies, in addition to maintaining the effectiveness of the Senate as a body.
  - It shall investigate questions of governance structures, maintenance of student government, and effective policy-making, and shall serve as a resource to other Senate committees to this end.
  - It shall investigate and lead initiatives relating to continuous internal maintenance of the Senate, and, in cooperation with the Vice Grand Marshal for External Affairs, other Senate committees, and other relevant bodies, shall investigate and pursue initiatives aimed to improve the Senate's effectiveness and accessibility.
  - It shall oversee the Senate's compliance with its Bylaws.
  - It shall investigate and recommend proposals for policies pertinent to the affairs of the Union, including pecuniary policy, within the Senate's legislative power vested to it by the Union Constitution.
  - This subclause shall not be construed as infringing upon the power of any committee of the Senate to investigate and recommend policy pertaining to its interest area. However, other committees or bodies within the Union may request the assistance of the Rules and Administration Committee in drafting policy or for input in issues of governance.
  - It shall be responsible for reviewing the language of all constitutional amendments that come before the Senate for approval.
  - It shall, as may be necessary, serve as an intermediary between the Senate and the Judicial Board on questions of a constitutional nature, and shall investigate parliamentary questions in cooperation with the Senate Parliamentarian.
  - It shall conduct a yearly review of Union policy, and provide recommendations to the Senate on ways to improve cohesiveness and clarity.
  - It shall be the chief custodian of historical Senate records.
  - It shall be charged with the notification of other bodies of the Union in all changes in Union policy passed by the Senate which are relevant to that particular body's operations or responsibilities.
  - It shall investigate the status of Union affairs as it pertains to compliance with Union policy, and inform the Senate of all pertinent information on this subject.
- To insert "for Internal Affairs" after "Vice Grand Marshal" in Article XIII, Clause 1.
